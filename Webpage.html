                                                                                                Get free replays for all players and team staff members
                                                                                                
                        
                                                                                                The replay package gives players and team 
staff members free access to replayes from all matches with the team 
(regardless of who has been filming). This covers only the replays and 
not the livestreams, and only for team members who have connected to 
their player or team staff role with their mobile phone number.
                                                                                                
                        
                                                                                                
                        
                                                                                                The replay package cost is 2 000 SEK per year
 (80 SEK per person in a team with 20 players and 5 team staff) and is 
paid using Swish. The team staff member/admin who pays gets a receipt to
 be able to get the money back from the club.
