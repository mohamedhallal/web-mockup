FORMAT 1 - 16:9 with CTA

Format description

Use image or video to show off your product, service or brand and amplify your message with text to encourage people to learn more about it. Video will auto play muted.

Technical requirements

  1. Primary headline: max 25 characters
  2. Image or video in ratio 16:9
    • Image: PNG or JPG, resolution 1280 x 720 px
    • Video: MP4, resolution 1280 x 720 px, 1500 kbps
  3. Logo, PNG or JPG, resolution 300 x 300 px
  4. Secondary headline: max 30 characters
  5. CTA headline: max 25 characters
  6. URL
